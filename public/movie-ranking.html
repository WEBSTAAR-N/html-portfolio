Friendship in Islam

Top 3 friendship advice from the Sunnah.

The qualities of a good friend?

"Your best friend is one who: seeing him reminds you of Allah, speaking to him increases your knowledge and his actions remind you of the hereafter." (Al-Muhasabi)

The seriousness of friendship

"A man follows the religion of his friend so each one should consider whom he makes his friend." (Abu Dawud)

What to do when your friend does something wrong

"If you wish to mention the faults of your friend, mention your own faults first." (Bukhari)
